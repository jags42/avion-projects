WAKANDA FOREVER

Chadwick Boseman

November 29, 1976 - August 28, 2020

Chadwick Boseman was an acclaimed American actor, born on November 29, 1976. He gained fame for his roles as Jackie Robinson in "42" (2013), James Brown in "Get on Up" (2014), and as T'Challa/Black Panther in the Marvel Cinematic Universe. He continued to work despite being diagnosed with colon cancer in 2016 and passed away on August 28, 2020, at age 43. Boseman is celebrated for his impactful performances and contributions to cultural representation in film.
